Overview

When planning a trip, it is hard to figure out what sites to see, restaurants to dine at, and the order and route to conduct these activities most efficiently. With Travel Pro, we hope to solve these hard-to-tackle intricacies and help inexperienced travelers perfect their itineraries. We allow them to personalize and share their experiences and influence others’ travels in the future.

Solution + Features

This app can be used by anyone. A user can be a 21-year-old traveling inexpensively by himself or a family of 5 traveling around the world with a considerable budget. With our app, users can create their perfect itinerary. They input their preferences, such as dates, budget and previous reservations to integrate, and receive a day-by-day itinerary that satisfies all of their travel needs. Users can also edit their itinerary to change various activities and timings. There is also a social element to Travel Pro as users can share their itinerary on social media, such as Facebook and Instagram, or on our very own “Itinerary Newsfeed.”

  • Browse through other’s itineraries
  • Favorite and gain inspiration from similar travels
  • Edit previous plans based on other’s plans
